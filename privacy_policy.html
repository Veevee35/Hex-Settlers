Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 14, 2026

This policy explains how Hex Settlers (the “Service”) handles information when you use the browser game or its desktop wrapper.

Information we process

  • Account data: username, display name, account timestamps, and game statistics. Passwords are stored only as salted password hashes.
  • Session data: hashed session tokens and expiration/usage timestamps. Browser session tokens are placed in HTTP-only cookies and are not exposed to browser scripts.
  • Game data: room membership, game state, rules, chat messages, completed-game history, and content or metadata submitted through custom texture-pack features.
  • Technical data: the server processes IP addresses and request headers to establish connections, enforce authentication limits, and protect the Service. The hosting provider may keep its own access or security logs.

How information is used

We use this information to authenticate players, run multiplayer rooms, restore interrupted games, display history and leaderboards, deliver user-selected content, prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and improve gameplay and AI behavior. We do not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising.

Storage and retention

Account and aggregate-stat records remain until the operator removes them. The Service keeps a bounded completed-game history and recoverable active-room snapshots; disconnected rooms may be removed after an inactivity period. Hosting backups can retain older copies for a limited time. Neural AI parameters and aggregate training metadata do not intentionally contain account credentials.

Cookies and local storage

An HTTP-only, same-site cookie maintains your login. Browser local storage holds interface preferences, audio settings, custom content preferences, and the last room code. Older releases stored a session token in local storage; the current client migrates that token into the protected cookie and removes the local copy.

Sharing and service providers

Information is disclosed only as needed to operate the Service, comply with law, protect users or the Service, or complete a business transfer. Hosting, networking, source-distribution, and desktop-release providers may process technical data under their own terms.

Your choices

You can log out to revoke the current browser session, avoid sending chat or custom content, and clear local browser storage. Contact the Service operator to request access to or deletion of account information, subject to applicable legal and security requirements.

Children and international use

The Service is not directed to children under 13. Do not use it if local law requires you to be older without parental permission. Information may be processed in the United States or wherever the hosting provider operates.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and will revise the date above. Questions or privacy requests may be sent to privacy@hexisles.com.
